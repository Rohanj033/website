Our Dedicated Team

Our dedicated team comprises of competent people with extensive knowledge and experience. Our professionalism and expertise help us to service our clients to their satisfaction. The key members of the firm are:

CA Rohan Jain

Rohan is a certified Chartered Accountant, having earned his qualification in 2021. With over three years of professional experience, he brings expertise in assurance, regulatory compliance, financial consulting, and both direct and indirect taxation. As a management consultant and corporate advisor, he provides strategic guidance in audit, taxation, and financial matters. His core strengths lie in delivering actionable insights, ensuring adherence to regulatory standards, and helping businesses enhance their financial efficiency.

Advocate Shubham Jain

Shubham is an Advocate and having over 8+ years of experience in the field of Direct and indirect taxation He has a strong track record of providing legal guidance, compliance solutions, and strategic tax planning to businesses. His expertise extends to representing clients in tax litigations, handling assessments, and offering advisory services on various tax-related matters

CA Bhavish Singla

Bhavish is a qualified Chartered Accountant, having earned his certification in 2021. With over three years of experience, he specializes in audits and income tax matters. He has served as a consultant to several large organizations, offering his expertise in Ind AS and income tax. His professional experience includes involvement in tax audits, GST audits, statutory audits, and compliance with MCA regulations.
